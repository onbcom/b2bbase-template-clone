Foundation is built for lean B2B sales teams, solo reps, and startups who need powerful CRM tools without the enterprise bulk. Whether you’re managing a small pipeline, just starting to scale outreach, or transitioning from spreadsheets, Flowis Foundation gives you everything you need to build consistent sales momentum—fast.

With a clean interface, built-in pipeline tracking, and essential automation tools, the Foundation Plan helps you stay focused on closing deals—not managing complexity. Simple to adopt, easy to love.

What’s included:

  • Smarter Pipelines — Visual drag-and-drop boards to manage every deal stage with ease and clarity.
  • Custom Fields — Add key attributes to contacts, deals, and companies—so your data works the way you sell.
  • Real-Time Activity View — Monitor rep activity, call logs, and email history—all in one place.
  • Email & Meeting Logging — Auto-log interactions to keep everything organized and searchable.
  • CRM Automation (Basic) — Trigger tasks or notifications based on deal stage changes.
  • Standard Reporting — See deal progress and pipeline metrics at a glance.
  • Secure Cloud Hosting — Data encrypted at rest and in transit, with role-based permissions.
  • Support Access — Email-based support to help you get up and running with confidence.

Perfect for seed-stage startups and small teams who want simplicity, speed, and structure—without the learning curve.
